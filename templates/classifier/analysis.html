This tool provides automated analyses intended to approximate potential political stances expressed in text. Its assessments may be inaccurate or incomplete, and should not be treated as definitive judgments about an individual's beliefs, affiliations, or intentions.

Political language is often nuanced, context-dependent, and influenced by cultural, historical, or rhetorical factors the tool may not fully capture. Results may also be affected by biases present in training data or model limitations.

This tool is intended for informational and research purposes only. It should not be used for decisions affecting individuals' rights, opportunities, or access to services (including employment, housing, legal, or political participation decisions). Users are responsible for applying human review, critical judgment, and, when appropriate, expert consultation.
