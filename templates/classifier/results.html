🔬 Methodology

Standard Models:

Fine-tuned transformer models (based on ManifestoBERTA) trained on political texts to predict positions across three key dimensions:

  • Left-Right: Economic and social policy preferences
  • Liberal-Illiberal: Attitudes toward democratic institutions and pluralism
  • Populism: Anti-establishment rhetoric and "people vs. elite" framing

Scores range from 1 to 10, with higher values indicating stronger positions.

  • Theory-Grounded: Based on established political science frameworks

Scores range from 0 to 10 with confidence intervals indicating prediction certainty.
